Executive Brief

Global FDI is projected to reach $9.2T by 2050, growing at 5.8% CAGR from 2025. Technology (+72%) and Renewable Energy (+68%) will account for 65% of total investment growth. Asia-Pacific leads with 22% CAGR, followed by MENA at 18%, driven by sovereign wealth diversification and green transition.
