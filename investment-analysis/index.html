GOSA Methodology

The Global Opportunity Scoring Algorithm (GOSA) is a 4-layer composite methodology scoring economies 0-100.

GOSA = (0.30×L1) + (0.20×L2) + (0.25×L3) + (0.25×L4)
L1Doing Business (30%)

World Bank methodology — 10 indicators across regulatory efficiency

L2Sector Intelligence (20%)

Sector-level scoring across 9 investment categories

L3Investment Zones (25%)

SEZ/FTZ availability, incentives, and infrastructure

L4Market Intelligence (25%)

Real-time signals from 1000+ official sources
